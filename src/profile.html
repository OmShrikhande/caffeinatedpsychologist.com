Welcome to the PsyCoffee Workshop, a unique space dedicated to brewing conversations around mental well-being and breaking down the stigma surrounding mental health.

I believe that just like a good cup of coffee, fostering mental clarity and emotional awareness requires a welcoming environment and shared understanding.

Led by me, Dipti, a passionate psychologist and mental health professional, my mission is to increase mental health awareness within our community. I recognize the significant lack of access to quality mental health care and strive to bridge this gap by providing clear and accessible information on mental health basics through interactive workshops held right here in the cafe.

Through my PsyCoffee Workshops, I aim to:

  • Break down harmful stereotypes and promote understanding of various mental health conditions.
  • Cultivate clarity of thoughts and emotional awareness in individuals through guided discussions and practical exercises.
  • Provide accessible resources and support in a relaxed and comfortable cafe setting.
  • Facilitate open and honest conversations about mental health, making it easier for everyone to share and learn.

I believe that everyone deserves access to mental health support and that open conversations are crucial for creating a more compassionate and understanding society. Join me on this journey towards better mental well-being, one workshop at a time.
